// FAQ

Straight answers.

Is this paid?

No. Operational Team is unpaid, full-time volunteer work. Executive Team is paid, but you don't apply there directly. See the path above.

How many hours a week?

Around 5-8 hours in normal weeks, more around events. Event PMs can hit 15-20 hours in the two weeks before their event.

When can I move to Executive?

You can apply after one year in Operational. Two years is what we look for in practice. And only when a seat opens, which won't happen before 2027 at the earliest.

What happens after the one-year mandate?

Three options at the end of your mandate: we renew (you stay another year), you apply for Executive (if there's a seat), or you exit gracefully. Nothing is automatic in either direction.

Do I need to work in tech?

Not necessarily. We care about what you can do, not your job title. If you're strong in design, comms, event management, or ops, apply. If you're already in tech, even better for the tech-heavy roles.

What if I don't get picked?

You stay in the volunteer pool. You'll get invited to help at events, and you can apply again next year when we open partial recruitment.
